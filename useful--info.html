Travelling to Canada

The closest airport to the venue is Ottawa international airport. There are then a range of local taxis and car hire options for getting to downtown Ottawa. Other airport options include Toronto Pearson or Montreal airport where you can then take a train or rent a car to get down to Ottawa itself.

Our recommendation would be to think about what you would like to do around the wedding as this may effect which airport you fly into.

Visa's

If travelling from abroad the likehood is that you will need a visa to enter Canada.  This is not a difficult process but is something you should do before travelling.

Where to stay in Ottawa

For those coming from outside Ottawa there are many different options for local accommodation. There are plenty of hotels, apartments and ‘Airbnb’ options in and around Ottawa area. These will vary in size and location but should be flexible enough to meet everybody’s needs.

Suggested places to focus on are areas such as Downtown, Glebe, Market, Sandy Hill. If in doubt or if you have any questions please feel free to drop us an email.

Local transport

There are a number of different ways to get around the city of Ottawa. By far the easiest is to car pool or to rent a car. Alternatively there is also the local bus network and the usual car share services such as Uber.
